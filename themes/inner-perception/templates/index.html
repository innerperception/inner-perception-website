About

Formed in 2023, Inner Perception brings a fresh voice to the music scene. With their intense energy and unique blend of post-hardcore with melodic hardcore influences, Inner Perception create soundscapes that are both powerful and memorable. The band's music is characterized by dynamic arrangements and soulful melodies, resulting in tracks that leave a lasting impression on listeners.
